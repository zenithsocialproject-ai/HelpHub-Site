How It Works in 4 Simple Steps

1. Scan QR / Fill Form

Users access a quick intake form via QR or they can access it via our website. This information will collect their needs, contact information, and zip code. All of which is used to match them with the appropriate resources.

2. AI Analyzes

Our AI engine identifies what help is needed and where. It uses the current list of social service providers in the area, takes their services and matches to the users need and zip code.

3. Matches via SMS

Tailored provider info sent directly to the user. It provides them with three resources and their contact information all via SMS.

4. Feedback via SMS

Users can interact with and adjust or explore more options. The AI is able to memorize which options work and dont work using their phone number memory.
