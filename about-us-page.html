Our Mission 🎯

At HelpHub, we believe that access to essential social services shouldn't be complicated. Our mission is to break down barriers and connect individuals in need with local social service providers through the simplicity of text messaging.

Using cutting-edge AI technology, we've created a platform that understands human needs and matches them with the right resources instantly. No apps to download. No complicated forms. Just simple, human-centered technology that works for everyone. People who are searching for resources are already strained on time and stress. Tracking down resources that meet individual’s needs is a very frustrating and broken system. HelpHub uses Artificial Intelligence to match individuals with the exact help they need. These needs are put through an algorithm that can train itself based on user feedback to improve itself.

Our Story 📖

The Problem We Saw

We noticed that countless individuals struggle to find the social services they desperately need. Complex websites, long wait times, and fragmented systems create barriers when people are at their most vulnerable. We knew there had to be a better way.

The Solution We Built

HelpHub was born from a simple idea: what if getting help was as easy as sending a text? We combined AI technology with a deep understanding of social services to create a platform that works for everyone, on any phone, anywhere. As a social service provider and case manager we saw how painful it was to get people struggling the effective help and or treatment they need in order to be successful in life. Although this isnt a magical cure all, we believe in this new technology to provide that solution.

Our Impact Today

Today, we've connected thousands of individuals with the services they need. From housing assistance to healthcare access, from food security to mental health support, we're making a real difference in communities across the country.
